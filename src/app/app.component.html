About MELI:Thriving in the industry for nineteen years, our team consists of over five hundred employees who possess high level of professional expertise and strive to specialize in a single field; teaching English. Our vision is to enable a maximum number of people to speak the international language, the international way. As English is the lingua franca of today’s world, we take a great pride in teaching it.

Today’s MELI:Across our 10 branches, we host tens of thousands of students! Within our 250 classrooms, MELI offers up to 240 new classes in a single week under the supervision of hundreds of English language specialists.

Tomorrow’s MELI:A significant international project of MELI, ourenglish.com, aspires to be the largest online English language learning platform in the world.  In this enormous endeavor, resources from the USA, India, and UAE are directly being put into practice to develop hundreds of specialized English language courses.
